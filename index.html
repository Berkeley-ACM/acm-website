About

With focus on the Computer Sciences, we promote education and interest in computing through knowledge sharing, fostering communication, and community involvement.

Started in 2023, Berkeley's ACM Chapter is officially chartered by ACM's Chief Operating Officer, and is part of the growing network of ACM chapters worldwide.
